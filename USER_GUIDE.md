# User Guide: New Widget and Navigation System

## Overview
This update introduces a redesigned widget system and improved navigation to make attendance tracking more intuitive and efficient.

## New Widget Options

You now have **three widget options** to choose from when adding widgets to your home screen:

### 1. Compact Widget (2x2)
**Best for**: Quick overview without taking much space
- **Size**: 2 tiles wide √ó 2 tiles tall
- **Features**:
  - Read-only display (no input buttons)
  - Shows up to 2 subjects
  - Displays attendance percentage for each subject
  - Color-coded: Green (above target) or Red (below target)
  - Dark theme support (AMOLED compatible)

**When to use**: When you want a small widget that shows your most important subjects at a glance without interacting with it.

### 2. Large Widget (5x2)
**Best for**: Quick attendance marking without opening the app
- **Size**: 5 tiles wide √ó 2 tiles tall (full width)
- **Features**:
  - Interactive attendance marking
  - Shows up to 5 subjects
  - Quick action buttons: P (Present), A (Absent), NC (No Class)
  - Displays current attendance percentage
  - Scrollable if you have more than 5 subjects
  - Updates immediately when you mark attendance
  - Dark theme support (AMOLED compatible)

**When to use**: When you want to quickly mark attendance directly from your home screen without opening the app.

### 3. Original Widget (3x2)
**Best for**: Balanced view with moderate detail
- **Size**: 3 tiles wide √ó 2 tiles tall
- **Features**:
  - Read-only display
  - Shows up to 3 subjects
  - Displays attendance count (e.g., "15/20 attended")
  - Shows percentage with color coding
  - Dark theme support (AMOLED compatible)

**When to use**: When you want more detail than the compact widget but don't need interaction buttons.

## New Navigation System

### Home Screen Changes

The home screen is now **cleaner and more focused**:

1. **Date Header**: Shows today's date in a colored card at the top
2. **Subject Cards**: Each subject is displayed in a card with:
   - Subject name and visual hint "Tap to view calendar"
   - Large pie chart showing attendance percentage
   - Attendance statistics (Present/Absent/Total)
   - Helpful guidance (e.g., "You can bunk 2 classes" or "Attend next 3 classes")
   - Quick attendance buttons (P/A/NC) for today

### Per-Subject Calendar

**New feature**: Each subject now has its own dedicated calendar!

**How to access**:
1. From the home screen, **tap on any subject card**
2. You'll see a calendar view for that specific subject
3. The calendar shows only that subject's attendance history

**Features**:
- View attendance history for the selected subject
- Color-coded dates (Green = Present, Red = Absent, Gray = No Class)
- Select any date to mark/change attendance
- Quick action buttons to mark attendance for selected date
- Back button returns to home screen

**Why this is better**:
- Focused view per subject
- Easier to track individual subject attendance
- Less cluttered than showing all subjects on one calendar
- Faster to find specific dates for a subject

## Settings Screen Changes

The Settings screen is now **simpler and more focused**:

**What's there**:
- **Customizations**: Change themes and colors
- **About**: App information

**What's removed**:
- Subject attendance targets (moved to Subjects tab)
- Subject list from settings

**Why this change**:
- Settings now contains only app-level preferences
- Subject management is in the Subjects tab where it logically belongs
- Cleaner, less confusing interface

## How to Use the New System

### Daily Attendance Marking

**Option 1: From Home Screen**
1. Open the app
2. Find the subject on the home screen
3. Tap P (Present), A (Absent), or NC (No Class) on the subject card

**Option 2: From Large Widget**
1. Don't even open the app!
2. Find the large widget on your home screen
3. Tap the P/A/NC buttons directly on the widget

**Option 3: From Subject Calendar**
1. Tap on a subject card
2. Select any date on the calendar
3. Mark attendance using the buttons below

### Viewing Attendance History

1. Tap any subject card on the home screen
2. Browse the calendar to see attendance for past dates
3. Color-coded dates show at a glance: Green (Present), Red (Absent), Gray (No Class)
4. Tap any date to change attendance if needed

### Managing Subjects

1. Go to **Subjects** tab (bottom navigation)
2. Add new subjects with the + button
3. Edit subject names or attendance counts by tapping on them
4. Configure attendance targets (required percentage)

## Tips for Best Experience

1. **Use Multiple Widgets**: Add both compact and large widgets
   - Compact widget at the top for overview
   - Large widget in the middle for quick actions

2. **Home Screen Workflow**: 
   - Quick check: Glance at widgets
   - Mark today's attendance: Use large widget or home screen
   - Review history: Tap subject card to see calendar

3. **End of Day Routine**:
   - Open the app or use the large widget
   - Mark attendance for all classes
   - Check if you're above/below target

4. **Planning Ahead**:
   - Tap subject cards to view calendars
   - See patterns in your attendance
   - Plan which classes you can skip based on the guidance shown
